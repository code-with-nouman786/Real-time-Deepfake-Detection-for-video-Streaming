Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "video check"?

A video check is counted each time our system analyzes a video frame. For live streams, we analyze multiple frames per second depending on your configuration. Short videos under 1 minute typically use 5-15 checks.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the higher plan's features. When downgrading, changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of our Professional plan with no credit card required. You'll get access to all Professional features with a limit of 1,000 video checks during the trial period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and PayPal. Enterprise customers can also pay via bank transfer.
